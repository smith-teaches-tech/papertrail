Six metrics. Research-backed. Entirely private.

  • Function Word Profile (35%) — Burrows' Delta; cosine similarity over 30 most common function words
  • Punctuation Fingerprint (20%) — comma, semicolon, em-dash rates per 1,000 characters
  • Sentence Length + Rhythm (15%) — average and standard deviation; Coh-Metrix methodology
  • Type-Token Ratio (15%) — vocabulary richness; stable per individual writer
  • Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level (10%) — reading complexity consistency
  • Informality Markers (5%) — contraction rate and first-person pronoun use
  • All computation runs locally — no text is ever transmitted
  • Minimum 500 words per sample for reliable results
  • Printable PDF report with full research citations
